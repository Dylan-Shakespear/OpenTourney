About OpenTourney

OpenTourney uses a very simplistic display, allowing you to quickly create and edit tournaments. Then, if the tournament is set to be public, others can view the tournament and see information relating to the matches and dates posted for said matches. As of now there are 4 different tournament sizes and 2 different tournament types.

Our Tournaments

Anyone is able to view public tournaments on OpenTourney, and anyone who is logged in can quickly create a new tournament and edit information relating to that tournament.

Search for "Public"

The search bar at the top will allow anyone visiting the page, whether logged in or not, to search through all public tournaments and review those respective tournaments. If you are logged in, the search will also expand to contain both all public tournaments and your private tournaments. When logged in, you can also click the "Tournaments" link at the top of the page to view all your public and private tournaments.

Create Tournament

When logged in, you can easily and quickly create a new tournament by clicking "New Tourney" at the top of the page. Then, you can easily edit the name, description, and public/private setting later. When viewing your tournament, you can click on any match to edit the player names, select which player has won the match, and set a date for the match that anyone viewing the tournament can click on the match to see.

Single Elimination

Tournaments can be created with anywhere from 4 to 32 starting teams. Additionally, they can be created in either the Single Elimination or Double Elimination style. In the Double Elimination style, once a team loses, instead of disappearing from the tournament, they go to the loser's bracket. Upon losing in the loser's bracket, they are finally done for good. At the end of the tournament, the winner of the winner's bracket and the winner of the loser's bracket will play a game. Generally, if the winner of the winner's bracket loses, an additional match will be held between them and the winner of the loser's bracket since that would only be their first elimination. This is not visually reflected on the tournament display but is assumed.

Dark Theme

When logged in, you can click the settings option at the top of the screen to changed the display theme when viewing a tournament. This only will affect the tournament view, but will change the view to your specific theme when viewing any tournament. The rest of the website is unchanged.
